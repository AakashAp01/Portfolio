A Color Clash

- Color Clash is a fast-paced and exciting color-matching game that tests your speed and accuracy. The objective is simple: match the color of the rapidly changing box with the color you've chosen.

- To win, you must stop the game at the exact moment the background color of the box matches your chosen color.
